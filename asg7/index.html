Biography

Samwise Gamgee, affectionately known as Sam, is a gardener from Hobbiton who proved himself to be one of the most courageous and loyal hobbits in all of Middle-earth. Born into a family of gardeners who served Bag End, Sam accompanied Frodo Baggins on the perilous quest to destroy the One Ring. His unwavering determination, practical wisdom, and steadfast friendship were instrumental in saving Middle-earth from darkness. After the War of the Ring, Sam returned to the Shire where he married Rosie Cotton, raised thirteen children, and served seven terms as Mayor of the Shire, bringing prosperity and peace to his beloved homeland.
