About Me

Hi, I’m Ngoc Thien Nguyen (NT)! I’m really into digital art, storytelling, and design. I like playing around with web design, making interactive stories, and looking at how media connects with culture. My art style mixes bright colors and bold illustrations with little symbolic details. I like giving my work a playful look while still leaving some meaning for people to find.

When I’m not drawing or writing, I’m usually trying out new creative tools or learning more about tech. I’ve been getting into coding with HTML and CSS because it feels awesome to turn an idea into something that actually works on a website. For me, art and tech go together perfectly. One lets me imagine cool stuff, and the other lets me bring it to life online.
