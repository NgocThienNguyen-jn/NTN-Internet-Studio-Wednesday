About Us

K/DA is a virtual K-pop girl group created by Riot Games, the company behind League of Legends. The group debuted in 2018 with the song “POP/STARS”, performed by real artists (including members of (G)I-DLE, Madison Beer, and Jaira Burns) while the characters—Ahri, Akali, Evelynn, and Kai’Sa—appeared in animated music videos.
They blend gaming, music, and pop culture, gaining huge popularity both inside and outside the gaming community. Their songs mix K-pop, pop, and hip-hop, and they’ve been used to promote League of Legends events like the World Championships. K/DA is seen as an example of how virtual idols can cross into mainstream entertainment.
